Spherical World

The future of media experience lies within a socially connected virtual world. VR started almost 30 years ago, bloomed in the 90s, forgotten for a while and now its back, and thatâ€™s not where it ends. Wearable devices are emerging in all directions; electronics are becoming so small that they can be implanted into a human skin. The future of screens is a socially driven 360-degree immersion. Using a keyboard and a mouse in such a world makes no sense. To create a true immersion, one should use more natural user interfaces such as voice command and depth sensors for body tracking. By combining an Oculus Rift (VR heads-on display), Microsoft Kinect (depth sensor) and a natural voice command interface, we created a socially connected 360-degree immersive virtual world for media exploration and selection and big data manipulation and visualization.
