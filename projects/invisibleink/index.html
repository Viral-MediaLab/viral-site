Invisible Ink

The problem of having complete transparency and control over our digital identity is growing stronger as our lives become more dependent on online and digital services. What once was rightfully ours is now spread among uncountable entities across many locations. We enjoy the value we receive from services and therefore consent to share with them our secrets, but what are they doing with our digital footprint? Are they keeping it safe? Do we even trust them? We have built a platform that gives back people control and ownership over what belongs to them, their digital identity. Using a peer-to-peer network, blockchain technology and secured off-blockchain storage we made it possible and trust-less to keep track of our personal data and manage who has access to it, such a network that protects us from single point of failures due to its underlying distributed and decentralized nature.
