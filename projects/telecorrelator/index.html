Telecorrelator

The Tele-Correlator allows people to view multiple perspectives of real-time news events aligned by content as well as time. It reveals emphasis and timing and allows participants to discover points of view and important events. Currently, the tele-cor- relator aligns broadcast news from four sources to allow comparison of emphasis and time allocation by four broadcasters to a succession of events.
